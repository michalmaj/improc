# Benchmark Results — 2026-04-21

## Machine

- **CPU:** Apple M4 Pro
- **OS:** macOS 25.3.0
- **Compiler:** Homebrew Clang 22.1.1 (arm64-apple-darwin25.3.0)
- **Build type:** Release
- **OpenCV:** 4.8

## Zero-Overhead Suite

Acceptance criterion: wrapper overhead ≤ 5 ns per op.
Input: 480×640 BGR (or Gray for CLAHE).

All times are CPU-time means from 3 repetitions (`--benchmark_report_aggregates_only=true`).

| Op | Raw OpenCV (ns) | improc++ (ns) | Overhead (ns) | Status |
|---|---:|---:|---:|:---:|
| `Resize` | 85,932 | 86,483 | 551 | ❌ |
| `GaussianBlur` | 79,967 | 79,885 | -82 | ✅ |
| `GammaCorrection` | 71,439 | 75,414 | 3,975 | ❌ (LUT rebuild per call) |
| `CLAHE` | 461,678 | 476,750 | 15,072 | ❌ (cv::createCLAHE per call) |
| `BilateralFilter` | 1,377,701 | 1,383,603 | 5,902 | ❌ |
| `ToFloat32C3` | 56,717 | 57,230 | 513 | ❌ |
| `NormalizeTo` | 167,483 | 169,579 | 2,096 | ❌ |
| `WarpAffine` | 179,980 | 177,439 | -2,541 | ✅ |

> **Note:** GaussianBlur and WarpAffine overhead is negative (within measurement noise), reported as ~0 ns ✅.
> All other ops exceed the 5 ns threshold. However, as a fraction of total op time, overhead
> ranges from 0.4% (BilateralFilter, ToFloat32C3) to 5.6% (GammaCorrection), with CLAHE at 3.3%.
>
> For CLAHE, overhead is dominated by `cv::createCLAHE()` object creation per call in the current
> implementation, not `Image<>` wrapper cost. For GammaCorrection, overhead is LUT rebuild per call
> (the raw benchmark now caches the LUT; improc++ `GammaCorrection::operator()` still rebuilds it
> each call). Pure wrapper overhead (`Image<>` constructor) is ≤1.5 µs for the remaining ops.
>
> The 5 ns criterion may need revisiting for heavier ops where absolute ns overhead is large
> but percentage overhead is negligible. Overhead attribution for CLAHE and GammaCorrection points
> to implementation-level optimisation opportunities (cache the CLAHE object and LUT) rather than
> a fundamental cost of the `Image<>` wrapper itself.

## Throughput Suite

Acceptance criterion: ≥ 30 FPS on 1920×1080, single thread.

Pipeline: `BGR(1080p) → Resize(224×224) → CLAHE → GaussianBlur(3) → ToFloat32C3 → NormalizeTo(0,1)`

All times are CPU-time means from 3 repetitions.

| | Raw OpenCV (ms/frame) | improc++ (ms/frame) | improc++ FPS | Status |
|---|---:|---:|---:|:---:|
| ML Preprocessing | 0.445 | 0.451 | 2,219 | ✅ |

> **Note:** Both raw and improc++ pipelines achieve ~2,200 FPS single-threaded on Apple M4 Pro,
> far exceeding the 30 FPS criterion. The 1.3% overhead (0.006 ms) is within measurement noise
> (stddev ≈ 0.003 ms).
